Get Your Global Passport

Explore the World through Education

At the UOL (Department of International Qualifications), we believe that education is your passport to the world. With Pearson Degree Level 4 & 5 programs, you can embark on a transformative journey that opens doors to global opportunities, enriches your life, and prepares you for a future without boundaries.

What is a Pearson Degree Level 4 & 5?

Pearson Degree Level 4 & 5 programs are globally recognized qualifications that provide you with a solid academic foundation and practical skills. They are designed to prepare you for success in both higher education and the professional world.

Level 4

Equivalent to the first year of a bachelor's degree, it's your stepping stone towards higher education excellence.

Level 5

Comparable to the second year of a bachelor's degree, it deepens your knowledge and skills for career readiness.

Why Choose a Pearson Qualification?

Global Recognition

Pearson is a name trusted by educational institutions and employers worldwide. Your qualification will be recognized and respected no matter where your journey takes you.

Flexibility

Our programs offer flexibility, allowing you to study at your own pace and balance your education with other commitments.

Practical Skills

You'll gain hands-on skills that are directly applicable to your chosen field, giving you a competitive edge in the job market.

International Network

Join a global community of learners, educators, and professionals, expanding your horizons and building connections that last a lifetime.

Explore
