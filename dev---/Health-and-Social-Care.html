Introduction

Health and Social Care is designed to provide students with a comprehensive understanding of the healthcare industry and the broader social and ethical issues related to healthcare delivery. The importance of such a program can be highlighted in several ways.

Addressing Healthcare Needs:

The program equips students with the needs of individuals and communities.

Quality of Care:

Education in health and social care emphasizes the importance of providing high-quality care to patients and clients. Students will be trained to ensure that healthcare services are delivered with compassion, ethics, and professionalism

Health Promotion and Prevention:

The program typically includes coursework on health promotion and disease prevention, which is vital in reducing the burden on healthcare systems. Students will be well-equipped to promote healthy behaviors and lifestyles in their communities

Entry Requirements

UOL, recruits students of good achievement from all educational backgrounds. Proven previous exam results are the main criteria for UOL. International (Tier-4) students also need to show English language competence to secure admission.
